### 🇬🇧 English Version

**Taiwan Daily Insider** is the ultimate morning briefing designed exclusively for foreign professionals, expats, and Gold Card holders navigating life and business in Taiwan. 

Every morning, we deliver a concise, sharp, and insightful summary of the island's most critical news. From breaking economic trends and tech industry shifts—including TSMC and the semiconductor supply chain—to the daily social conversations buzzing on local platforms like PTT and Dcard, we've got you covered. 

Powered by cutting-edge AI automation, our podcast ensures you stay ahead of the curve, culturally connected, and fully informed on the topics that truly matter, all without the language barrier. 

Grab your morning coffee, and let's dive into what's shaping Taiwan today.
